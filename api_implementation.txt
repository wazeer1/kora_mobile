War Ground (Live Debate) Real-Time Updates
​

debate:timer_update -- War Ground (Timer Display, Time Sync)

debate:mic_change -- War Ground (Speaker Highlight, Queue Display, Mic Icon)

debate:view_mode_change -- War Ground (Grid ↔ Presentation Mode Switch)

team:chat_message -- War Ground (Team Strategy Chat Panel)

debate:hand_raised -- War Ground (Captain's Cockpit, Queue Update)

debate:speaker_yielded -- War Ground (Speaker Change, Queue Advance)

debate:participant_joined -- War Ground & Green Room (Roster Update, Hex Grid Update)

debate:evidence_added -- War Ground (Evidence Sidebar, Presentation List)

debate:sentiment_update -- War Ground (Tug-of-War Slider Animation)

debate:paused -- War Ground (Pause Overlay, Timer Freeze)

debate:resumed -- War Ground (Resume Overlay, Timer Resume)

debate:started -- Green Room → War Ground (Transition to Live, Init State)

debate:ended -- War Ground → Game Report (Debate Closure, Transition to Results)

debate:verdict_ready -- Game Report Screen (Show AI Analysis Results)

error -- All Screens (Error Notification, Disconnect Handling)
